About:

Robbyn Bentley Walters (b. December 28, 2001) is an American creator, animator, performer, musician, and writer. Known for his shenanigans and tomfoolery, he is regarded as a little goofy.

Robbyn graduated Owosso High School in 2020 and spent a year at College for Creative Studies in Detroit to study animation in 2021-22. In 2025, he recieved an Associate of Fine Arts in digital media and is currently working toward a Bachelor of Fine Arts in digital art and design at Kendall College of Art and Design of Ferris State University in Grand Rapids.

In the summers, Robbyn is a part of Parks and Recreation and has worked at two state parks: Sleepy Hollow (2023-2024) and P.J. Hoffmaster (2025-present). He is currently working toward becoming a park ranger. In his free time, the Robster enjoys making music, crocheting and getting outdoors! Recorded here on this website are all of his creative ventures and muses available for viewing pleasure!
