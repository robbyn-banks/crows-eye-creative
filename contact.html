About:

Robbyn Bentley Walters (b. December 28, 2001) is an American creator, animator, performer, musician, and writer. Known for his shenanigans and tomfoolery, he is regarded as a little goofy.

Robbyn attended Owosso High School and graduated in 2020. He went on to attend College for Creative Studies in Detroit to study Entertainment Arts: Animation in 2021-22. He recieved an Associate of Fine Arts in digital media and is currently studying Digital Art and Design at Kendall College of Art and Design of Ferris University in Grand Rapids.

In his free time, the Robster likes to perform on stage and in the pit. His most recent endeavors include playing keyboard, flute, and oboe at the Lebowsky Center. He also enjoys reading and drawing and generally being creative! Recorded here on this website are all of his ventures and muses available for viewing pleasure!
